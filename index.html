About

I am an AI algorithm researcher at Huawei working on AI4Code and multi-agent software-engineering agents — large-model code repair, issue resolution (SWE-Bench), and research-platform delivery. I designed CodeR, a task-graph multi-agent framework that reached state-of-the-art on SWE-Bench-Lite, and take frontier techniques from research breakthroughs into real industrial code repositories.

I received my M.S. in Natural Language Processing from Nankai University (2023) and my B.E. from Harbin Engineering University (2020). My work has appeared at ICSE, ACL, and TOSEM.

Research Interests
